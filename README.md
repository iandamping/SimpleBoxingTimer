# Simple Boxing Timer

A professional, high-performance, and beautifully designed boxing interval timer application tailored for fighters, coaches, and fitness enthusiasts. Whether you are training for boxing, MMA, Muay Thai, or high-intensity interval training (HIIT), this application provides highly reliable, millisecond-accurate round tracking.

## ✨ Features

* **Multi-Screen Adaptation (Portrait & Landscape):** Seamlessly transition between vertical and horizontal orientations. The layout dynamically restructures to maximize visual clarity, making it easy to track your time whether your device is standing upright on a phone mount or laying flat on the gym floor.
* **Beautified & Modern UI:** Designed with high-contrast typography and sleek structural elements. Optimized for long-distance visibility so you can confidently check remaining time in the middle of a heavy bag combination or sparring session.
* **Rock-Solid Warning System:** Features an enhanced, bug-free warning timer. Auditory signals and visual flashes transition flawlessly to notify you precisely when the round is coming to an end.
* **Fully Customizable Workouts:** Easily configure round durations, rest intervals, warning thresholds, and the total number of rounds to fit your specific training regimen.
